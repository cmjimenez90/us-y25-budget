Major Budget Functions

Functional areas like Defense (050), Health (550), Medicare (570), etc. show only placeholder amounts (no specific figures provided in the resolution).

Policy Highlights

1. Mandatory Spending Focus

  • Over 70% of the Federal budget is considered mandatory spending.
  • Committees are instructed to reduce costs in these programs as designated.

2. Economic Growth Emphasis

  • Targets growth through lower taxes, deregulation, and expanded energy production.
  • Seeks to boost workforce participation and GDP growth.

3. Reserve Fund

  • Allows Budget Committee Chair to adjust allocations if legislation meets reconciliation targets.

Quick Metrics Snapshot

  • Overall Required Cuts: $1.502T
  • Overall Allowed Adds: $4.8T
  • Net Effect: Potential for more deficit expansion than cuts, plus $4T added to the debt ceiling.

Purpose

Blueprint for FY 2025–2034: Directs each committee on targeted spending adjustments.
Reconciliation Process: Ensures alignment of enacted legislation with set fiscal priorities.
